Dyatlov Pass - A terrifying, yet intriguing case

Information about the case:

  • Total victims: 9.
  • Cause of death: 6 from hypothermia, 3 from physical trauma.
  • Location: Ural Mountains, Russia.

Other details:

  • The tent they were temporarily staying in was ripped open from the inside.
  • One body emitted radiation.
  • Some of the eyes and tongues had been eaten.

Theories:

  • Yeti; Some believe a Yeti was responsible, but that wouldn't explain the radiation. Does explain physical trauma.
  • Aliens; Somewhat-likely, explains radiation and physical trauma.
  • Government secret; Some think they stumbled upon something they shouldn't have seen, KGB got to them. Explains trauma, maybe radiation.
